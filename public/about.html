About Justice Connect

A modern e-Filing platform bringing transparency, efficiency, and accessibility to court complaint management across jurisdictions.

Our Mission

Justice Connect was built with a single purpose: to remove the barriers that prevent citizens from accessing justice. Traditional court filing processes involve long queues, mountains of paperwork, and opaque status updates. We change that by providing a secure, intuitive, web-based platform where anyone can file a complaint, track their case in real time, and communicate directly with court staff — all from their browser.

The system is designed to serve everyone in the legal process — from citizens filing their first complaint, to clerks managing intake queues, judges reviewing caseloads, and administrators overseeing the entire workflow.

📋 Seamless E-Filing Wizard

Our guided 4-step complaint wizard walks complainants through every detail needed for a valid court submission. No legal jargon, no confusing forms — just simple, structured steps.

  • Select complaint type and court jurisdiction
  • Fill in party details — complainant, respondent, and address
  • Enter case number and hearing date if already assigned
  • Write a detailed narrative of the dispute
  • Optionally attach evidence documents, photos, or PDFs (up to 10 MB)
  • Submit and receive an instant reference number

Each complaint is stamped with a unique reference ID, timestamped, and immediately visible to assigned staff — no delays, no lost paperwork.

⚖️ Real-Time Case Tracking

Once a complaint is filed, both the complainant and court staff can monitor every stage of its lifecycle. Status updates are reflected immediately on the dashboard without needing to refresh.

  • Filed — Complaint received and logged
  • Pending — Awaiting clerk review and assignment
  • In Progress — Active investigation or hearing scheduled
  • Under Review — Judge has taken up the matter
  • Scheduled — Hearing date confirmed
  • Resolved / Rejected / Closed — Final disposition recorded

A live chat timeline within each complaint allows complainants, clerks, and judges to exchange remarks, upload clarifications, and maintain a full audit trail.

🛠️ Multi-Role Staff Console

Justice Connect is not just a citizen portal — it is a full staff collaboration environment. Different roles see different views and controls, ensuring every user has exactly what they need without clutter.

  • Admins access global analytics, category breakdowns, and priority reports
  • Clerks manage the registry intake queue and assign cases
  • Judges view their personal caseload and issue rulings
  • All staff can update statuses, post remarks, edit details, and delete records
  • CSV export with optional remarks and attachment columns for audit compliance

Who Uses Justice Connect?

The platform supports four distinct roles, each with tailored dashboards and permissions:

🧑

Citizen

File new complaints, track case status, upload evidence, and communicate via the live remarks timeline.

📝

Registry Clerk

Review incoming filings, validate submissions, assign cases to judges, and manage the intake queue.

⚖️

Judge

Access assigned caseloads, schedule hearings, issue orders, and post official rulings.

🛠️

Administrator

Full system oversight — analytics, user management, export reports, and system configuration.

Technology Stack

Justice Connect is built with a lightweight, battle-tested stack that keeps the system fast, secure, and easy to deploy. The entire application can be hosted on Vercel's serverless infrastructure at zero cost during development.

Security is a first-class concern: all passwords are hashed with bcrypt, sessions are managed server-side with HTTP-only cookies, and every API endpoint enforces role-based access control. File uploads are validated for type and size before storage.
